Calculation Math Logic

1

Gross Basis: Total Sales (₹{{ "%.2f"|format(total_sales) }}) - Discount (₹{{ "%.2f"|format(total_discount) }}) = Taxable Base (₹{{ "%.2f"|format(taxable_amount) }})

2

Tax Calculation: Taxable Base (₹{{ "%.2f"|format(taxable_amount) }}) × 5% GST = ₹{{ "%.2f"|format(total_gst) }}

3

Net Result: Taxable Base + Total GST = Net Revenue (₹{{ "%.2f"|format(net_revenue) }})

* This logic ensures the owner only pays tax on the actual amount received after store discounts.
